//About SigmaBowl//

SigmaBowl is a project by Monocle and Scorpion5700. We could say that it's an online quizbowl application, and while it is that, it's really just a remake of Protobowl. It features numerous improvements, such as theme customization (WIP), more categories and control over what questions you get (WIP), an account system (WIP), and performance analysis (WIP).

To begin playing, click the "Play Now" button on the top and you'll be able to join a number of rooms. Hopefully as time goes on, the number of "WIP"s will go down.
